Baby Fluid Requirement Calculator

Important Considerations:

  • Premature Infants: Fluid requirements may be different and should be determined based on specific guidelines for preterm infants.
  • Medical Conditions: Infants with certain medical conditions may have different fluid needs, and healthcare providers will adjust volumes accordingly.
  • Expressed Breast Milk: Consider giving expressed breast milk based on the condition of the baby.
  • Frequency of Feeding: The total daily volume is divided by the number of feedings per day to determine the amount per feeding session.

Note: These guidelines provide a general overview and are not a substitute for professional medical advice.
